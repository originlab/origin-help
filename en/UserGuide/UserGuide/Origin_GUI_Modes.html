2.8 Origin GUI Modes

GUI-Modes

Origin offers 3 GUI modes when during installation:

  • Default mode: Origin's classic interface with complete menus, toolbars, buttons, and graphing options.
  • Stats mode: pre-installs a collection of Apps and enables an interface tailored for statistical analysis and quality-control workflows.
  • Life Science mode: pre-installs Apps and arranges the interface to streamline pharmacological and biological assay workflows (e.g., dose-response curves, ANOVA and survival analysis).
